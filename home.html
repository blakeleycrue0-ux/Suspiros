Who We Are

A private centre for addiction recovery.

Suspiros is a private residential centre for drug and alcohol addiction, based near Palma de Mallorca, Spain. We provide confidential, medically supervised treatment for adults who are ready to make a change, in a calm, structured environment built specifically for recovery.

Every resident is assessed individually before arrival, and receives a personalised treatment plan built around their own history and goals. Our approach combines medically assisted detox, individual and group therapy, and the 12-Step tradition, blended with holistic practices such as yoga and mindfulness — because we believe lasting recovery depends on treating the whole person, not just the addiction.

A multidisciplinary team of clinicians, therapists, and key workers is on site around the clock. From your first confidential enquiry through to your aftercare plan, you are never simply handed a schedule and left to follow it alone — the same team walks with you at every stage.

Suspiros is set in a private residential villa surrounded by orange groves and gardens, with a pool, a covered terrace, and quiet spaces for reflection alongside shared spaces for group work and connection. It is designed to feel like a home, not a hospital — private, unhurried, and away from the pressures of daily life.

Every enquiry and every stay is treated with complete confidentiality. Suspiros is designed for adults seeking residential treatment for drug or alcohol addiction — whether this is a first step, or a return after a previous attempt — and for the families who want to understand how to help.

How We Work

A clear, structured process.

No two residents follow the same path, but every path is built on the same five foundations.

A clinician manages your physical stabilisation safely, around the clock, before therapeutic work begins. Nothing is rushed, and nothing is left unsupervised.

Every resident is assessed individually and given a treatment plan built around their own history and goals, reviewed regularly with their key worker as they progress.

Our programme is rooted in the 12-Step tradition, blended with evidence-based therapy and a steady daily rhythm — not tied to any religion, and open to people of every background.

Where it is appropriate and wanted, family sessions can form part of the plan, and we keep loved ones informed throughout the stay.

Before you leave, we build your aftercare plan together, so your recovery continues with structure and support after you go home.

For Families

Support for the people who love them.

Addiction affects the whole family, not just the person in treatment. We keep you informed, welcome visits once your loved one has settled, and support regular calls and video contact throughout their stay.

Our admissions and family liaison team is your point of contact from the first enquiry, so you are never left wondering what happens next.

When someone you love needs help but can't see it yet.
