Daily Rhythm

A day at Suspiros.

No two days are identical, but every day follows the same steady shape — structure that supports recovery without feeling rigid.

Morning

Grounding and check-in

Mornings begin gently, with a wellness practice such as yoga or mindfulness, followed by a check-in with your key worker or clinical team.

Midday

Therapy and group work

The core of the day: individual sessions, daily group therapy, and workshops on relapse prevention, change, and emotional wellbeing.

Afternoon

Rest and reflection

Time to process the day, alone or with others — in the quiet room, the grounds, or the pool — alongside any scheduled clinical appointments.

Evening

Shared meals and connection

Residents share meals and time together, building the peer connection that is a core part of the 12-Step tradition, before a calm close to the day.

Admissions

What happens after you reach out.

01

Confidential Enquiry

You contact us by phone, email, or the form on this site. Every conversation is private, with no obligation, and you can reach out on your own behalf or for someone you are worried about.

02

Assessment

A clinician speaks with you to understand your situation and confirm that our programme is the right fit, and to answer any questions you or your family have before deciding anything.

03

Arrival

We arrange your travel and admission, and prepare your room and initial care plan before you arrive, so your first day is settled rather than uncertain.

04

Personalised Treatment

Detox if needed, then therapy, structure, and support — adjusted with you as you progress, through regular reviews with your key worker and clinical team.

05

Aftercare Planning

Before you leave, we build your aftercare plan together, so your recovery continues with structure and support — not a sudden return to how things were before.
