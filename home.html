Who We Are

A private centre for addiction recovery.

We treat drug and alcohol addiction with evidence-based care, in a structured and deeply human residential setting in Mallorca.

Every resident is assessed individually and given a personalised treatment plan. Our clinical team is on site around the clock, alongside a dedicated key worker who walks with you from your first day to your last.

We believe recovery is not only clinical. Alongside medical care, we nurture connection, routine, and self-understanding — the foundations of a life that lasts beyond treatment.

For Families

Support for the people who love them.

Addiction affects the whole family, not just the person in treatment. We keep you informed, welcome visits once your loved one has settled, and support regular calls and video contact throughout their stay.

Our admissions and family liaison team is your point of contact from the first enquiry, so you are never left wondering what happens next.
