Support for the people who love them.

Addiction affects the whole family, not just the person in treatment. We keep you informed, welcome visits once your loved one has settled, and support regular calls and video contact throughout their stay.

Our admissions and family liaison team is your point of contact from the first enquiry, so you are never left wondering what happens next.

Where it is appropriate and wanted, family sessions can form part of the treatment plan — recovery tends to hold better when the people closest to someone understand what changed and why.

When someone you love needs help but can't see it yet.

It is painful to watch someone you love struggle with addiction, especially when they cannot yet see it themselves. You do not have to face that alone. Our admissions and family liaison team can talk you through how to approach the conversation, with compassion and clarity, and help you understand what support is available — for them, and for you.
