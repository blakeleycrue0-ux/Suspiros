Back to Home Who We Are

A private centre for addiction recovery.

We treat drug and alcohol addiction with evidence-based care, in a structured and deeply human residential setting in Mallorca.

Every resident is assessed individually and given a personalised treatment plan. Our clinical team is on site around the clock, alongside a dedicated key worker who walks with you from your first day to your last.

We believe recovery is not only clinical. Alongside medical care, we nurture connection, routine, and self-understanding — the foundations of a life that lasts beyond treatment.

Suspiros is a residential setting, which means you live on site for the length of your programme rather than attending sessions and returning to daily life in between. Stepping fully away from familiar pressures and routines, in a calm and private environment, is part of how the programme works.

Who We Help

Adults ready for a different kind of support.

Drug and alcohol addiction

Our programme is designed for adults seeking residential treatment for dependence on alcohol or drugs, including situations where medically supervised detox is needed first.

People who want privacy

Many residents choose a private, residential setting precisely because confidentiality and discretion matter to them and their families.

Those who have tried before

Whether this is a first step or a return after a previous attempt, our team meets you where you are, without judgement.

Families seeking guidance

We also support the families of people struggling with addiction, helping them understand the process and how to help.

Our Story

Built on the twelve steps.

Suspiros was founded on a simple belief: recovery lasts when it is personal, structured, and never walked alone.

Our programme is rooted in the 12-Step tradition, blended with medical care, therapy, and a steady daily rhythm.

First developed in the 1930s, the 12-Step model remains one of the most respected and widely used recovery frameworks in the world. It is not tied to any religion, and welcomes people of every faith and background. At Suspiros, we offer it as one part of a broader, personalised pathway — alongside evidence-based treatment and close therapeutic support.

12-Step Foundation

A globally recognised framework for lasting recovery, offered alongside clinical and holistic care.

Our Approach

What guides how we work.

Privacy, always

Every enquiry, assessment, and stay is completely confidential. What happens at Suspiros stays between you and your care team.

A plan built around you

No two residents follow the same path. Your treatment plan is built with you, and adjusted as you progress.

Never walked alone

A dedicated key worker and clinical team are with you from your first enquiry through to your aftercare plan.

Recovery beyond treatment

We build routines, connection, and self-understanding that are designed to last well beyond your stay with us.

Evidence-based, not one-size-fits-all

We draw on established, evidence-based therapeutic approaches, but apply them flexibly — your programme is shaped around your circumstances, not a fixed template.

A calm, structured setting

Recovery is easier to sustain in an environment built for it. Our residential setting, daily rhythm, and small resident numbers are designed to support that.
