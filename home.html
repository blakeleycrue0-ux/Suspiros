Who we are

A sanctuary for lasting recovery

Suspiros is a private residential rehabilitation facility in the peaceful countryside of Mallorca. We offer evidence-based treatment for substance use disorders in a structured, supportive, and deeply human environment.

Our Mission

To provide safe, person-centred rehabilitation that addresses both the physical and emotional dimensions of addiction — combining clinical expertise with genuine compassion.

The Setting

Chosen for its tranquility and distance from triggering environments, our Mallorcan location supports healing through nature, structure, and community — far from the patterns that sustain addiction.

Our foundations

Built on four pillars

01

Clinical Safety

Comprehensive admission assessments, medical oversight, and structured detox protocols managed in partnership with licensed clinical professionals.

02

Therapeutic Depth

Daily group therapy, one-to-one sessions, 12-step work, and psychoeducational workshops tailored to each resident's needs and goals.

03

Community & Peer Support

Recovery happens in connection. Our peer-led check-ins, shared meals, and mutual encouragement build belonging at the heart of the programme.

04

Whole-Person Wellbeing

Mindfulness, yoga, outdoor recreation, and structured routines nurture the body and mind alongside therapeutic work.

Clinical approach

Treatment for the whole person

⚕

Medical Assessment & Detox

Every resident receives a comprehensive medical assessment on arrival. Personalised detoxification and medication plans are created and overseen by licensed clinical professionals. All medication is stored securely and administered under supervision.

◎

12-Step Recovery Model

The 12-Step framework — first developed by the founders of Alcoholics Anonymous — provides a structured pathway of acceptance, accountability, and personal growth. It is integrated into daily life and group work throughout the programme.

◈

Group & Individual Therapy

Daily group sessions build community and emotional self-awareness. Weekly one-to-one sessions with a key worker allow for deeper personal exploration. Fortnightly psychologist sessions are also available.

❋

Holistic Wellbeing

Mindfulness, meditation, yoga, and outdoor activities support emotional and physical health. Recovery is not only about the mind — it is about rebuilding a whole and fulfilling life.

The twelve steps

A structured path
to lasting freedom

The 12-Step model offers a respected, internationally adopted framework for recovery. At Suspiros, it forms one important part of a broader, holistic treatment pathway tailored to each individual.

01 Acceptance

Acknowledging powerlessness over addiction and recognising that life has become unmanageable.

02 Trust

Coming to believe that a power greater than ourselves can restore us to wellbeing.

03 Liberation

Making a decision to turn our will and our lives over to the care of a Higher Power.

04 Understanding

Taking a searching and fearless moral inventory of ourselves.

05 Acknowledgement

Admitting to ourselves and another human being the exact nature of our wrongs.

06 Freedom

Becoming ready to have our defects of character removed.

07 Growth

Humbly asking for our shortcomings to be removed.

08 Reflection

Making a list of all persons we have harmed and becoming willing to make amends.

09 Forgiveness

Making direct amends wherever possible, except when doing so would cause further harm.

10 Continuity

Continuing to take personal inventory and promptly admitting when we are wrong.

11 Connection

Seeking through prayer and meditation to deepen conscious contact with our Higher Power.

12 Helping Others

Having had a spiritual awakening, carrying this message to others and practising these principles in all our affairs.

Getting started

The admission process

Every step of our admissions process is designed to ensure you receive the right care, at the right time, in a safe and appropriate environment.

1

Initial Enquiry

Contact us directly, through a family member, or via a professional referrer. We gather basic information and identify immediate needs.

2

Telephone Assessment

A trained admissions team member conducts a comprehensive assessment covering dependency, treatment history, physical and mental health, and risk.

3

Clinical Review

The management and clinical team review the case to confirm appropriate placement, capacity, and the ability to safely meet the individual's needs.

4

Admission Planning

Once approved, an admission date is confirmed. We coordinate arrival logistics, pre-admission guidance, and all required documentation.

5

Medical Assessment on Arrival

A full medical assessment is conducted on the day of arrival, either at a private clinic or on-site. A personalised detox and medication plan is created.

6

Programme Begins

Once stabilised and safely admitted, your structured rehabilitation programme starts. You are welcomed into the community and supported every step of the way.

What to bring

  • Comfortable clothing (no nightwear for communal areas)
  • Toiletries and personal hygiene items
  • Any prescribed medication in original packaging
  • A small amount of personal cash (stored securely on arrival)
  • Books, journals, or personal items that support wellbeing

What we ask of you

  • A commitment to engage fully with the programme
  • Willingness to participate in group sessions and daily duties
  • Honesty with staff, peers, and yourself
  • Respect for the shared community and environment
  • Openness to a new way of living

Family & loved ones

  • Visits available for programmes of 8 weeks or more (after week four)
  • Phone calls permitted from 24–48 hours after arrival
  • Scheduled calls on Tuesday, Friday, and Sunday evenings
  • Weekend FaceTime with family available
  • All visits must be pre-arranged with management
